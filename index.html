Public speaking , words that would certainly put fear in the heart of many people. Speaking in public is nothing but a piece of cake for those whose who are confident enough to present themselves in front of hundreds or even thousands but at the same time it's like a nightmare for those who are less experienced on this matter. They tend to lack the ability to tackle the fears that comes while speaking in front of a large audience mass.

Here are some ways how you can overcome these fears while public speaking and present your topic flawlessly

1.Know your topic throughly

While doing any kind of presentation you should be well aware of the subject your are speaking at. You should research about your topic and master it in a way how you find it easy to present. Google it,ask for a friend and many more making sure that you are credible to your audience. This will make your audience feel like you are the right person for the presentation

2. Feel confident

Before you start a presentation make sure that you are comfortable and you are good to go with your presentation. Don't feel even remotely anxious. Take a deep breath , think positively and organize your work and deliever giving your every best. You should also have backup plans in case anything goes wrong regarding computer,projector,your slides and things like that.

3.Make eye contact with the audience

While presenting your topic you should keep an eye contact with your audience. When you keep an eye contact with your audience they will feel engaged with the topic. One of the biggest mistakes people make while presenting any topic is not keeping an eye contact with the audience resulting in the disconnection between the speaker and the audience. The main purpose of keeping an eye contact with the audience is to make sure you are giving the proper attention to them.
